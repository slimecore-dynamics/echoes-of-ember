Placeholder Images Needed for First-Person View
=================================================

Create these images in: game/images/exploration/first_person/

Required Images:
----------------
1. wall.png (800x600) - Gray stone wall texture
2. floor.png (800x600) - Dark floor texture
3. ceiling.png (800x600) - Stone ceiling texture
4. door_closed.png (800x600) - Wooden door (closed)
5. door_open.png (800x600) - Open doorway
6. corner.png (800x600) - Corner where two walls meet
7. interact_highlight.png (100x100) - Glowing indicator for interactions (animated if possible)

For now, the system will use colored rectangles as placeholders:
- Wall: #666666 (gray)
- Floor: #333333 (dark gray)
- Ceiling: #4D4D4D (medium gray)
- Door Closed: #8B4513 (brown)
- Door Open: #D2B48C (tan)
- Interact Highlight: #FFFF00 (yellow, pulsing)
